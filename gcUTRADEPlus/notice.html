Important Notice:-

Please be informed that CIMB will impose a penalty of RM100 or 1% of the value of the subsequent purchase made by CIMB (to cover for the failed delivery) whichever is higher, for non-delivery of Non Ringgit shares. This penalty is in addition to any other penalty charges that may be imposed by other country's exchanges which have buying in mechanism for non delivery of shares.

The penalty will take effect from 15/04/09.
