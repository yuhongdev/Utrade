Important Note:

Requested withdrawals of your selling proceeds are subject to approval. Upon approval, withdrawal requests will be subjected to 3 business days processing. Checks from approved withdrawals (without Special Instructions) will be deposited directly to the Bank Settlement Account as indicated in the Online Trading Account Application Form, upon submission.

If you have not been able to indicate a Bank Settlement Account in your forms and wish to do so, please email us at deposits@unicapital-inc.com. Please note that we will be using checks from BDO or BPI. As such, checks of clients with accounts from different banks may be subjected to clearing.

For Special Instructions (SI), kindly email us at deposits@unicapital-inc.com with the subject: "SI - Withdrawal Request for (your account code)". Your account code can be located at the top right corner of the page beside your name.

Please take note that we will only be processing withdrawal requests above P500.00.
