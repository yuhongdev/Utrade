Good Till Date Expiry Note

IMPORTANT MESSAGE

Please take note that your Good Till Date (GTD) Order(s) will automatically be cancelled when the following events happen:

  • The contract value of your GTD order(s) is more than your available trading limit.
  • The specific counter in relation to your GTD order(s) is suspended from trading by Bursa.
  • The order price of the specific counter in relation to your GTD order(s) is outside the range of "Upper & Lower Limit Price".
  • For sell order, the quantity of your GTD order(s) is more than available free shares maintained in your portfolio account. (eg capital reduction)
