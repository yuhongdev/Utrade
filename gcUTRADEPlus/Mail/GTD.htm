Dear Valued Clients,

 

Please take note that "Good Till Date" (GTD) order instructions via i*Trade@CIMB are automatically cancelled when the counters concerned are suspended from trading. New order instructions must to be keyed in again upon upliftment of the counter from suspension.

 

Hence, we wish to remind everyone who has keyed in GTD orders to BUY or SELL CIMB shares prior to the suspension of CIMB counter on 11 January 2005 to re-key in your orders if you wish to continue with the trade in CIMB shares when the suspension is uplifted.

 

Please be guided accordingly.
