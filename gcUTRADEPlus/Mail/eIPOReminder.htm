CIMB e-IPO service is exclusive to i*Trade@CIMB clients holding a direct CDS account with CIMB Securities registered under their personal name.

Why CIMB e-IPO?

In addition to trading on Bursa Malaysia via www.itradecimb.com; you can now enjoy the convenience of applying for new shares from your PC by clicking on www.eipocimb.com. This service is available to you anytime, anywhere.

CIMB e-IPO provides you an alternative to conventional methods of applying for shares. This service provides you with access to information on the latest IPO offered by CIMB, offers you a more efficient share application process online, and making payment via online funds transfer from Malayan Banking Berhad (presently the sole authorised financial institution) via www.maybank2u.com.my. More importantly, CIMB e-IPO will provide auto notification of balloted results to all e-IPO applicants via email.
